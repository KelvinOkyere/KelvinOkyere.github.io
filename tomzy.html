Unveiling the Songwriting Sensation from Lagos to London

In the dynamic world of entertainment, the spotlight often shines on emerging talents whose journeys are nothing short of extraordinary. Today, we have the privilege of introducing you to a rising star, Tomzy, whose real name is Omotomiwa Kehinde-Osems. Hailing from the bustling streets of Lagos and now making waves in London, Tomzy is a gifted songwriter and performing artist, ready to captivate audiences worldwide.

Tomzy's journey into the world of entertainment began with a harmonious twist. Around 2004, in the vibrant neighborhood of Surulere, he discovered his remarkable vocal talents. His enchanting voice led him to perform at his primary school's end-of-year parties year after year, solidifying his reputation as a young talent to watch. As the eldest sibling, Tomzy naturally assumed the role of spokesperson for his peers, allowing him to express emotions and experiences that others found difficult to articulate. This early foundation sowed the seeds of his storytelling and musical career.

Tomzy's artistic voyage has been punctuated by significant achievements and remarkable milestones. Notably, he became an integral part of the CREATIVE WICK MUSIC INCUBATORS, a platform that enabled him to nurture his craft. His musical journey has also seen him perform with GLITCH AFRICA and grace the stage at STRATFORD CROSS, where he mesmerized audiences with his powerful performances. To add to his list of accomplishments, Tomzy released an Extended Play (EP) titled "GOOD BOY GONE BAD," which showcased his versatility and creativity.
A turning point in Tomzy's artistic journey arrived when he secured writing deals with A-list artists. This shift in perspective changed his outlook from "if" to "when," instilling confidence in his path within the music industry.

What sets Tomzy apart is his ability to convey profound emotions and vibrations when tackling various themes in his music. His songs have a magnetic quality that resonates deeply with listeners, touching on subjects that often remain unspoken. For avid fans and enthusiasts, Tomzy has exciting news to share. The deluxe version of his project, "GOOD BOY GONE BAD," is set for release in January 2024. This release promises to be a musical journey that delves even deeper into his artistry.

At the core of Tomzy's artistry lies the theme of unity and shared experiences. Through his music, he seeks to remind his audience that no one navigates life's challenges in isolation, irrespective of the subject matter. Tomzy draws inspiration from a diverse array of artists, including Wizkid, 2face, Burna Boy, Wande Coal, Don Jazzy, Olamide, and international talents like Kanye West, Sia, and Billie Eilish. These influences contribute to the rich tapestry of his musical style.

Tomzy's creative process is as diverse as his music. He begins his day by immersing himself in music from a wide range of artists to set the mood. Inspiration can strike at any moment, leading him to record phrases or words during odd hours. For Tomzy, creativity is a lifestyle, and he embraces the ever-changing nature of the process.

Criticism and being thrust into contests before he was fully prepared have been significant challenges in Tomzy's career. However, these experiences have molded him into the artist he is today. Tomzy's fans might be surprised to learn that he is an excellent cook. He hints at plans to showcase his culinary talents in the near future, adding an exciting dimension to his multifaceted personality.

Tomzy's advice for aspiring artists is rooted in the power of mindset. He emphasizes that maintaining a positive mindset is essential, as it can carry you through both good and challenging days in the entertainment industry.

He encourages artists to stay true to their feelings and emotions, as authenticity is a driving force behind his own success.
To stay creatively inspired and motivated, Tomzy simply lives life to the fullest. He finds inspiration in everyday experiences and remains in constant motion.

In closing, Tomzy invites readers to explore his project "GOOD BOY GONE BAD," a musical representation of his love life and a journey through his unique artistry. As Tomzy continues to evolve and captivate audiences, we eagerly await his upcoming releases and the exciting chapters of his musical career.

Stay tuned for more from this rising star who brings a blend of Lagos and London to the global stage. Tomzy is a name you'll want to remember.
