Arathejay: From Ghana to Kenya, Spreading Positivity and Revolutionizing the New Afrobeats Scene

Born on December 21st in Sekondi-Takoradi, Ghana, Arathejay is a gifted music artist who embodies the rich tapestry of African culture and music. Although he currently calls Tema, Ghana, home, his early years in Kenya left an indelible mark on his artistry. This unique blend of experiences has shaped him into a rising sensation in the Ghanaian music scene, with the potential to make a lasting impact on the evolving Afrobeats landscape.

Arathejay's musical journey began as a young boy in Ghana, where he was exposed to the vibrant rhythms and melodies that define the country's musical heritage. However, it was during his time in Kenya that he discovered the power of cultural diversity in music. The fusion of Ghanaian and Kenyan influences ignited a creative spark within him, laying the foundation for his distinctive sound.

One of Arathejay's standout achievements to date is his chart-topping single, "Sankofa." This track not only showcases his lyrical prowess but also exemplifies his commitment to spreading positivity through music. "Sankofa" is a celebration of African heritage and the importance of looking back to move forward, a message that resonates with audiences far and wide. The remix collaboration with the renowned Ghanaian superstar, King Promise, catapulted Arathejay into the spotlight, further solidifying his position as a rising star to watch.
