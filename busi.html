There is no art without communication. That is the crux of Soul Sessions: An experience that highlights the VERSE in converse. Here, artists embrace the space afforded to perform and then ford the audience’s inquisitive waters during its ensuing Q&A. Soul Session’s essence encapsulates both the creative and philosophical nature of what it means to be an artist and how they coexist together.

Busi brings forth a warm and inviting nature that encourages the artist’s ease to delve deep into what fuels them. As they share their art’s design, it informs the audience of their past interactions, internal inspirations and how they translate into art. The audience gains a glimpse into the artist’s inner sanctuary, by listening and responding with reflections of their own. Busi’s effervescence streamlines the give-and-take between audience and artist as she creates a space for deeper introspection through expression.

Though most people stave off the intensity and vulnerability that comes with soulful symposium, Busi’s cajoling and comedic nature braves it, welcomes it and even yearns for it. She doesn’t adhere to the blinders of conformity and pulls this contrarian approach off with her goofy sincerity. She doesn’t take herself too seriously as she’s too “busi” regarding her guests sincerely. A maverick in her magnetism, she’s able to befriend any stranger and through Soul Sessions, she furthers her purpose to ascertain everyone’s experiences and enrich each other’s perspectives as a collective.


The heart of Soul Sessions incarnates the conviviality of conversation and communication as an art. In doing so, it materializes its own artistry as a means of connection.

Soul Sessions: Art meets Philosophy Soul Sessions is a live performance plus artist interview series, curated by Soulisically Speaking. Soulistically Speaking means having a deep and meaningful conversation. Think of it as a thread to help us navigate the path to understanding the human connection. Derived from the word “soul,” which in culture is often represented by the spiritual essence of human existence, it is about a thorough exploration of that essence. It is intentional communication, taking a deeper look at our true nature, our motives, reasoning and getting to a place of better understanding ourselves and each other. - Busi
